New York Style Cheesecake

Description:

With this recipe, a creamy, gorgeous, crack-free New York-style cheesecake is totally doable, even for beginners!

Ingredients:

  • Graham crackers, crushed
  • Cream cheese
  • White sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sour cream
  • Vanilla extract
  • Milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter together in a medium bowl until well combined; press onto the bottom of the prepared pan to make a packed crust.
  3. Mix cream cheese and sugar together in a large bowl until smooth. Blend in milk, then mix in eggs, one at a time, until just combined.
  4. Add sour cream, flour, and vanilla; mix until smooth. Pour filling onto prepared crust.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour. Turn the oven off; let cheesecake cool in the oven with the door closed for 5 to 6 hours to prevent cracking.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.
